School of Business Management (SBM) is a premier school of Noida International University offering holistic, skill-based, and value driven education to its students. The school is committed to excellence, backed with highly qualified and motivated faculty with wide experience in industry, research, and consultancy. The school focuses on imparting education for transforming the students into leaders and competent professionals ready to accept challenges in this competitive world.
SBM offers the most innovative and industry relevant management programs PG (Like MBA in Data Analytics) , and Doctoral levels, which are designed as per the NEP 2020 Guidelines. These MBA in Data Analytics programs equip the students with best of the content knowledge along with the specified skill set required by Industry. SBM also offers industry integrated programs in collaboration with various top companies of the world. These MBA in Data Analytics programs are flexible in nature and provide a wide range of specializations and elective subjects, which leads to the greater interest among students.
The post pandemic scenario across the globe necessitates a new category of global managers that will not only contribute to the development of their organization but also contribute towards societal development in order to make this world a better place to live. The School of Business Management focuses on imparting value-based education through innovative pedagogy and advanced curriculum.
Since, knowledge cannot be imparted effectively within the four walls of the classroom. Therefore, we provide adequate exposure to our students by organizing various events, industrial visits, outbound activities, the Summer Internship Program and Invited Talks by eminent personalities from Industry and Academia. We have also introduced a Mentor- Mentee Programme that aims at preparing our students for the rigours of professional life, thus ensuring the overall development of their personalities.
I appreciate the efforts of our qualified and dedicated faculty members and staff for aligning with the vision of NIU and putting their team efforts into the growth of students and the institution. We are sure that we will continue to develop the youth of the nation and give them the right direction for their future.
• Advanced Curriculum catering to the needs of Industry 4.0
• Academic and research excellence through highly qualified and experienced faculty.
• Flexible Academic Programs with Choice Based Credit System
• Innovative teaching pedagogy comprising experiential learning, simulations and case-based approach
• Access to Harvard Business Publishing learning materials and resources
• International Collaborations for students exchange and research activities
• Tie up with Top Companies for value added certifications.
• Industry Exposure through visits, expert talks and Internship projects
• Regular PDP and Career Counselling session by renowned experts
• Holistic development of students through various Clubs/ Committee activities
• Excellent Internship and Placement Opportunities
To be a premier institution in business education dedicated to cultivating innovative thinkers and leaders. We aim to empower students through a dynamic curriculum and cutting-edge teaching methods, fostering a culture of creativity and excellence that prepares them to excel in a rapidly evolving global marketplace.
• M1. To cultivate well-rounded business professionals with the knowledge, skills, and critical thinking abilities needed to thrive in today’s dynamic and competitive marketplace.
• M2. To foster a learning environment that encourages critical thinking, creativity, and problem-solving, inspiring students to become ethical and socially responsible leaders.
• M3. To prepare students to operate effectively in a diverse and interconnected world by comprehensively understanding global business practices and cultural nuances.
• M4. To instill a strong sense of ethical responsibility and sustainability in students, enabling them to make informed decisions that contribute to a better future
Our Graduates will have a comprehensive understanding of global business practices and cultural diversity, preparing them to operate effectively in an international business environment.
Our Graduates will demonstrate effective leadership and management skills, enabling them to lead teams and organizations in a dynamic business environment.
Our Graduates will cultivate an entrepreneurial mindset, fostering innovation and creativity to identify and capitalize on new business opportunities.
Our Graduates will understand the ethical implications of business decisions and will be committed to social responsibility, sustainability, and ethical practices in their professional endeavors.
Our Graduates will be able to think critically and strategically, making informed decisions based on quantitative and qualitative analysis to solve complex business problems.
