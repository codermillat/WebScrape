The MBA Dual Degree Program in Logistics & Supply Chain Management is a prestigious international collaboration between Noida International University (India) and MUST University (Malaysia), designed for ambitious professionals seeking a global edge. This program combines academic excellence with hands-on international exposure, equipping students with the skills, knowledge and global perspective needed to thrive in today’s dynamic logistics landscape. With the global logistics market valued at $9.7 trillion in 2024, projected to surpass $13 trillion by 2028 and India’s logistics sector expected to hit $380 billion by 2025 and with a 10–12% CAGR, the industry offers immense career opportunities. Key growth drivers such as e-commerce boom, infrastructure reforms and rapid adoption of automation, AI, IoT and tech-enabled operations make this the perfect time to enter the field—and this program the perfect launchpad for your global career.
• Dual MBA Degree recognized in India & Malaysia
• Real global exposure with a paid internship abroad
• ISCEA Global Certification (optional, USA-based)
• Industry-integrated curriculum focused on practical outcomes
• Opportunity for part-time work in Malaysia during semester breaks
• 100% Placement Assistance.
• Part-time work rights for students in Malaysia (up to 20 hours/week during semester breaks with immigration approval)
• The global logistics & supply chain market is worth $9.7 trillion (2024)
• Expected to exceed $13 trillion by 2028
• India’s logistics sector to reach $380 billion by 2025 with 10–12% CAGR
• Growth drivers include:
• – Infrastructure & logistics reforms
• – Automation, AI, IoT & tech-enabled operations
• Supply Chain Analyst / Manager

• Global Trade Compliance Officer

• Retail, E-Commerce & FMCG
• International Trade & Shipping
• Work-integrated learning in Malaysia
• Opportunity to gain cross-border career exposure
• Build international networks for long-term growth
