Humanities & Social Sciences
The graduate Humanities & Social Sciences programmes at Sharda University covers a wide range of disciplines with differing methodologies, from highly qualitative analysis of history to political science and the analysis of the material culture and thought of past societies. The programmes challenge the students to think critically and incisively about ideas, people, society and the human condition.
Our dedicated faculty enlightens students with broader perspectives, external fields and technology competencies. In addition to this, the faculty prepares students for an excellent career in varied fields including professional study and research. The programmes strive to bring excellence in students through a personalized academic environment that cherishes holistic knowledge and inspires critical thinking.
