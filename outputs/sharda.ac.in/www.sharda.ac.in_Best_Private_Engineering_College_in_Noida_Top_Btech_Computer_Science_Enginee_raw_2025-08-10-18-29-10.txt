Sharda School of Computing Science & Engineering is an open platform for diverse voices where teaching runs parallel to the real world and students are groomed to join the global workforce. SSCSE is distinguished as one of the top-ranked engineering schools in India. The students at SSCSE benefit through the professional grooming of renowned faculty and industry experts having experience of tackling pressing engineering problems. Students discover their passion in one of the various offered Engineering majors at the Sharda School of Computing Science & Engineering.
A student-centric pedagogy, project-based approach and design-driven curriculum provides students with an inclination for complex problem solving, design, innovation, and a passion for learning.
Vision of School of Computing Science & Engineering
To become a global institute of higher learning in Computing Science and Engineering, promoting excellence in research, innovation, and entrepreneurship to provide sustainable solutions for the challenges of progressive society.
Mission of School of Computing Science & Engineering
• To impart quality education with strong industry and academia collaborations in the expanding and allied fields of Computing Science & Engineering, fostering a conducive and transformative learning environment.
• To produce eco-conscious socially and ethically responsible technocrats equipped with professional skills and experiential learning, to empower them for adopting emerging technologies.
• To inculcate a culture of multidisciplinary research, disruptive innovation and accelerated entrepreneurship, while providing sustainable solutions to address multifaceted challenges of growing society.
• To promote collaborative learning and critical thinking through effective mentoring and counseling, with the aim of creating future leaders to serve communities worldwide.

The goal of school, guided by the vision, is to provide transformational education that produces a skilled workforce of researchers and innovators of tomorrow with intellectual and technological resources. Our commitment and dedication is to focus on experiential, cooperative and project-based learning that allows our students to continue to adapt, grow and succeed in solving real-world problems. The school thrives to establish a partnership with industries, government organizations, & academia and become a collaborative community of faculties, students, staff and alumni to fulfil societal needs with professional ethics. The school is proactively involved in Outcome-Based Education & Choice Based Credit System to enhance inter-disciplinary competence and leadership skills among our students in a global perspective.
Sharda School of Computing Science & Engineering is comprised of seven departments – Bio-Technology, Civil Engineering, Computer Science & Engineering, Electrical and Electronics & Communication Engineering, Mechanical Engineering. Each department offers undergraduate, postgraduate & PhD programs and is also running different domain-specific specialized programs to fulfil the needs of skilled professionals as per market demand.
SSCSE is having an excellent learning environment with multimedia driven lecture halls including lecture capturing facility and State-of-the-art laboratories and other facilities to support student learning, faculty research and academic success throughout all our academic programs. Many of these labs and centres of excellence are having collaborations with industries. The school runs different foundation/bridge courses that are required to fill the gaps among students coming from diversity and geographical areas and create a strong foundation.
Our experienced faculties from premier institutes bring a wealth of knowledge and expertise to the classroom. Their vast industry and teaching experience coupled with their research, and skill-based training by industry experts allow our students to not only receive professional teaching but also the opportunity to work alongside their faculties on research areas of their interest.
SSCSE is also having very good Industry Academia linkages with industries/associations like IBM, Inurture, Aries Communications, Aruba, Tech Mahindra, NASSCOM, NSDC, Savitri Telecom Services etc. apart from the academic tie-ups with top global Universities.
The school facilitates students in creating different clubs and societies/students-chapters at school/department level under the supervision of faculties for their holistic development through Experiential Learning. Students are helped in organizing different extra-curricular, technical activities and industrial visit etc. where all students can participate in fulfilling their dreams and broaden their horizons outside classrooms. School also offer to fund students projects under university project funding policy, support students in higher studies and in different time bounded International exchange programs with leading Global universities abroad under credit transfer system.
