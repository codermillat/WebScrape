Master of Science (Medical Microbiology)
School of Medical Sciences & Research (SMS&R)
SMS0502
3 Years
• Course Fee
M. Sc Medical Microbiology is a three-year post-graduate programme dedicated to cover the medical and molecular aspects of immunology, mycology, virology, and bacteriology to infection. The programme equips students with the knowledge of diagnosing and treating diseases caused by bacteria, fungi and parasites. The offered programme combines lectures, seminars and practical laboratory classes, culminating in a laboratory-based research project.
The curriculum of the programme offers a significant level of theoretical and practical understanding of the subject, which aids students in following a career in clinical sciences or academic and industrial research. Final assessments are made based on their performances. Graduates of this programme will be able to contribute to evaluation, planning and implementation at different levels of the healthcare system.
This course is for individuals who...
have analytical and problem-solving skills. It is for candidates who have the ability to communicate and encourage awareness about microbiological concepts effectively.
Students who are looking for...
obtaining a fulfilling career dedicated to improving the healthcare industry for the betterment of society must opt for this programme.
