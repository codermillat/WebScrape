• Course Fee
MD in Psychiatry at Sharda University is a three-year programme divided into six semesters specialising in the study of the prevention, diagnosis, and treatment of mental, addictive, and emotional disorders. The programme helps to enlighten students on the medical, psychological, and social components of mental, emotional, and behavioural disorders using a wide range of treatment modalities, including diagnostic tests, prescribing medications, psychotherapy. During the programme, students gain fundamental knowledge on neurobiology, biopsychology, prescription of psychiatric drugs and diagnosis and treatment of psychiatric disorders.
The programme is career orienting in nature that allows students to have simplified and constructive knowledge regarding the field of Psychiatry and its other branch domains. It trains students to treat patients through counselling, psychotherapy, and if required, electric convulsive therapy (ECT) and the administration of drugs. The curriculum also includes training on desensitising patients, mainly through hypnosis, for treatment of mental problems, such as depression and schizophrenia. The curriculum involves study on subjects like Psychology, anatomy, physiology, Biochemistry of the brain, Neurology and neurosurgery, Child Psychiatry, Community Psychiatry, Social Psychiatry, Forensic Psychiatry, Preventive Psychiatry.
This course is for individuals who...
Are compassionate about providing holistic care to their patients are an ideal fit for this programme. Excellent communication skills and attention to detail are some of the skills that can ensure success in this field.
Students who are looking for...
Acquiring fundamental knowledge of the necessary skills required in psychiatry and behavioural sciences. Students who want to give back to the society through their diagnostic, therapeutic and rehabilitative services should opt for this programme.
