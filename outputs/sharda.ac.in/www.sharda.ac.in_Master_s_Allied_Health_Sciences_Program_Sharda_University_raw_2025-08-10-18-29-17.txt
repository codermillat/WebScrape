The allied health sciences masters programmes at Sharda University imparts students with expertise in diagnostic, preventive, curative, therapeutic and rehabilitative interventions. The diversified curriculum provides a basic understanding of human diseases, health administration, ageing and wellness. The coursework is flexible, and students can choose from any areas that interest them.
The allied health sciences degrees provide ample opportunity to students to meet industry requirements for physical therapy, medicine, dentistry, veterinary medicine, occupational therapy, physician assistant and public health. The programmes also equip students with a comprehensive overview of the health care delivery system allowing them to gain industry exposure in areas such as pharmaceutical and medical equipment sales, health education, and health information services. These post-graduate programmes are a pathway to becoming an accredited health expert.
