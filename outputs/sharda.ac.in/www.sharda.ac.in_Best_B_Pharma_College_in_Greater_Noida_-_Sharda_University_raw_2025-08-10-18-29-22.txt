The graduate Pharmacy programmes at Sharda University aims to train students with high-level international standards to serve as health advisors in the community. The programmes offer world-renowned education to build foundational and professional skills with hands-on rotational assignments, projects, and intensive leadership skills training. Under these programmes, students learn about the process of designing, developing and formulating drugs.
In addition, students also gain knowledge about how diseases affect normal human biology and which medications are effective in preventing or treating those diseases. The programmes offer a balanced practical and theory hours for students' to learn and practice what they have learned. The constantly evolving curriculum equips the students with the latest pharmaceutical, biomedical, social and clinical science, and the practical skills to advance tomorrow's patient care.
