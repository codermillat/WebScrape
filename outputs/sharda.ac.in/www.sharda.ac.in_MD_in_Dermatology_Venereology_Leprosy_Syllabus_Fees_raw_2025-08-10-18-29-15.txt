MD-Dermatology, Venereology & Leprosy
School of Medical Sciences & Research (SMS&R)
SMS1301
3 Years
• Course Fee
MD in Dermatology at Sharda University is a three-year postgraduate programme that trains students in the advanced study of skin- related diseases. This programme deals with the diagnosis and treatment of diseases regarding skin, nails, and hair like cancer, eczema, psoriasis, acne, skin allergies and nail infections. The main goal of this programme is to equip students in diagnosing and managing common skin diseases, manage all medical emergencies related with skin, leprosy and venereal disease efficiently, adopt preventive measures against infectious diseases, acquire the requisite knowledge and laboratory skills and adopt a compassionate attitude towards patients (and their families)
The curriculum of MD in Dermatology programme at Sharda University involves a comprehensive blend of Presentations, Industry Collaborations, Weekly seminars etc. Topics usually covered in the curriculum are Physiology, Bio-Chemistry, Pathology, Bacteriology, Mycology, Virology, Parasitology, Pharmacology, Infections, Bullous Disorders, Papulo Squamous, Connective Tissue Disorders, Vascular Disorders, Disorders of Appendages etc. After obtaining the postgraduate degree, students can opt for pursuing further education in the specialization of their choice.
This course is for individuals who...
require a fundamental understanding of various skin related diseases and procedures like cryotherapy biopsies should opt for this programme. Anyone with a good academic record and a desire to succeed is an ideal fit for this course. Excellent communication skills with networking skills are added skills that can ensure success in this field.
Students who are looking for...
A lucrative career opportunity revolving around diagnosis and treatment of skin and other related areas like skin cancer, melanomas, moles and skin tumours should opt for this programme.
