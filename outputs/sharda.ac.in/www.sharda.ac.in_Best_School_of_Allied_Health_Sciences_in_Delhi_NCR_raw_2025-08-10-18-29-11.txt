Sharda School of Allied Health Sciences plays an essential role in the modern health environment. The School is committed to provide the highest quality learning environment to its students by way of class room teaching, clinical & community exposure clubbed with discussions, seminars & journal clubs for latest advances in the respective field, guided tours & externships to prestigious institutions. Highly trained professionals from the school, with its associated thousand bedded hospital, after successful completion of the course, contribute in high-quality healthcare services of the country & abroad, by practicing and applying the knowledge acquired. Their hands on experience with patients or the community arms them with the knowledge of the latest & prepares them for the challenges of healthcare industry.

Dear Students and Aspirants, Welcome to the world of Allied Health Sciences—a field where science, technology, and compassion converge to transform healthcare delivery. Allied health Sciences is a broad field that helps in fostering and supporting overall patient care and the healthcare system. They are essential to the worldwide healthcare system because they offer a variety of therapeutic, behavioral, supportive, and diagnostic services. In todays rapidly evolving healthcare landscape, the role of allied health professionals has become more crucial than ever. They are the professionals required in supporting diagnosis, treatment, and rehabilitation with specialized expertise. From medical technologists ensuring accurate lab results, to physiotherapists aiding in recovery, and radiology technologists providing critical imaging insights, each profession within Allied Health Sciences is vital to patient care. As healthcare continues to evolve, allied health professional is an essential part of healthcare teams worldwide and will keep on growing in importance and relevance.
We are committed to providing a comprehensive education that blends theoretical knowledge with practical training. Skill development is very much focused by the utilizing our state-of-the-art laboratories, clinical affiliations, and dedicated faculty ensure that our students are well-prepared to meet the demands of the healthcare industry. We focus not only on academic excellence but also on developing critical thinking, empathy, and ethical practices in healthcare.
India needs over 6 million allied health professionals to meet the current demand and the World Health Organization (WHO) recommends a ratio of one Allied health professional per thousand population, but India falls significantly short of this benchmark. Hence a career in Allied Health Sciences offers diverse opportunities, from working in hospitals, diagnostic centers, and rehabilitation clinics to engaging in research, public health, and healthcare management. Entrepreneurship and opening our own ventures are also opted my many students. As the global demand for skilled healthcare professionals continues to grow, graduates of Allied Health Sciences are well-positioned for rewarding careers both in India and abroad.
I encourage you to explore the various programs we offer and embark on a journey that promises both personal fulfillment and professional success. Together, let us strive for excellence in education, skill improvement, innovation, and a commitment to serving humanity. Best wishes for a successful and impactful career in Allied Health Sciences!
Wishing you the best and bright future.
