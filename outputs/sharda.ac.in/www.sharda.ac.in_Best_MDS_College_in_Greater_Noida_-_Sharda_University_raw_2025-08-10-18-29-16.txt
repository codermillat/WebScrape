The post-graduate dental programmes at Sharda University focus on providing in-depth knowledge of medicine that deals with the conditions related to the teeth, jaw and oral medicine. The programmes help in improving student's skills and understanding in diagnosing and treating patient's gums. During the programmes, students learn to identify diseases of the mouth and perform orthopaedic surgery.
The programmes provide a perfect blend of conventional and contemporary dental education, thus, striving for excellence in teaching and oral health services. Post completion of the programmes, the students are expected to find opportunities in academics, hospitals, research establishments and even may take up entrepreneurship. With experience, they should be able to take up leadership positions in their chosen career.
• Master of Dental Surgery (Paedodontics & Preventive Dentistry)
• Master of Dental Surgery (Orthodontics & Dentofacial Orthopedics)
• Master of Dental Surgery (Periodontology)
• Master of Dental Surgery (Oral & Maxillofacial Surgery)
• Master of Dental Surgery (Conservative Dentistry & Endodontics)
• Master of Dental Surgery (Prosthodontics and Crown & Bridge)
• Master of Dental Surgery (Oral Pathology & Microbiology)
