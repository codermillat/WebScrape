BBA LL.B.(Hons.) Integrated
SOL0101
5 Years
• Course Fee
B.B.A. LL.B.(Hons.) Integrated is a five-year undergraduate programme focusing on the study of commerce & business administration along with the in-depth study of law & legislature. The programme is structured in a way to train students for the business world with expertise in corporate laws and regulatory affairs.
The curriculum of the programme includes subjects like Marketing Management Organisational Behaviour Cost & Management Accounting Environmental Law Corporate Law and Jurisprudence.
Students will gain valuable insights in the business functions like Finance International Business Marketing Human Resource Management and understanding of corporate laws in the fields of Investment Banking Competition Corporate Governance Insurance and Mergers and Acquisitions.
Other than this the major advantage of this programme is that it creates ample opportunities for its aspirants. Students can easily switch their role according to their area of interests. The case studies guest lectures and industrial visits included in the programme give students closer insight into the functioning of corporate law in the business world.
Programme Educational Objectives (PEO)
• PEO1: To Ensure Conceptual Knowledge
• PEO2: To Inculcate Good Communication and Interpretation Skills
• PEO3: To Develop Critical Analytical Skills and Practical Application
• PEO4: To Promote Legal Research
• PEO5: To Provide Free Legal Aid and to develop Socially Conscious Professionalism
• PEO6: To create quality future Legal Professionals
• PO1: Sound Subject Knowledge: Good knowledge and comprehension of the core information associated with the legal profession.
• PO2: Communication: Effective Oral and Written Communication.
• PO3: Practical Application Skills: Drafting, Mooting, Client Counseling, Debating, Trial Advocacy, ADR, and skills related to management studies.
• PO4: Critical Thinking: Possess quality of critical legal thinking by Moot Court and Judgment reading especially minority judgments by applying the principles of scientific inquiry, thinking analytically, clearly, and critically, while solving socio-legal issues. Find, analyze, evaluate, and apply knowledge systematically.
• PO5: Research Tools: Ability to use Research Tools such as Library and e-resources including SCC online and West Law etc.
• PO6: Entrepreneurship: Possess relevant necessary required skills for Legal Professionals
This course is for individuals who...
are resilient confident and outstanding in communicating with others. This programme is for students who value ethics law and principles and are keen to learn how law regulates the business entities.
Students who are looking for...
professional business skills in the early stage of their career. It is for aspirants who seek to work for spaces combining management and law primarily in corporate domain.
