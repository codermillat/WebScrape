Executive MBA
SBS0147
Two years
• Course Fee
The Executive MBA program at Sharda School of Business Studies is a specialized course designed for those working in Government & Commercial organizations, Private firms, Hospitals, Institutions, etc. and are willing to enhance their managerial skills and business competencies. It is also well-suited for self-employed professionals, family businesses and budding entrepreneurs. The Executive MBA program shall be conducted in flexible hybrid mode on weekends and will therefore enable learning while continuing with the existing profession. This program will enhance the innovative, entrepreneurial and leadership capabilities for delivering efficient and effective value to the organization and society.
• Two years including Industry projects
• Classes to be held in blended mode on weekends
• Executive MBA in Marketing and Sales Management
• Executive MBA in Human Resource Management
• Executive MBA in Finance
• Executive MBA in Supply Chain and Logistics Management
• Executive MBA in Health Care and Hospital Administration
• Executive MBA in Entrepreneurship and Family Business
• Executive MBA in Public Policy and Administration
• Executive MBA in International Business
* Subject to minimum 8-10 students in each specialization
This course is for individuals who...
• Working Professionals with a minimum of two years full-time job experience in a registered firm, educational institutions, government organization, hospitals, start-ups, etc.
• Graduate or equivalent degree from a recognized university in the fields viz. Management, Engineering, Art, Science, Commerce, Law, Pharma, etc. with minimum 50% aggregate marks
Students who are looking for...
• To enhance and upgrade managerial skills in diversified specialization areas.
• To become professional experts with strong principles and awareness of societal and ecological challenges
