School of Medical Sciences & Research (SMS&R)
SMS2102
3 Years
• Course Fee
A master's degree in obstetrics and gynecology is a two-year postgraduate programme. Obstetrics and Gynecology is a medical specialty that deals with all aspects of obstetrics and gynecology through a single postgraduate degree. This combined training equips students to care for female reproductive organs and handle obstetric difficulties, even if surgery is required.
The programme equips students with the clinical information, procedural skills, and attitudes necessary to handle pregnancy and its difficulties, as well as abnormalities of the female genital tract and issues related to human reproduction.
