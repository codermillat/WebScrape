The graduate Basic Sciences programmes at Sharda University provides a strong scientific foundation for the students to excel in the field of natural health arts and sciences. The programmes included in basic science are aimed to enlighten students with the scientific theories for improved understanding or prediction of natural and other phenomena. The curriculum is designed to meet the distinct competencies required for careers in Biotechnology, Botany, Microbiology, Biochemistry, Physics, Mathematics, etc.
The basic sciences faculty is trained in disciplines including organic chemistry, biochemistry, anatomy, physiology, cell and molecular biology, immunology, microbiology, pharmacology and pathology and is dedicated to quality teaching and mentoring. The university encourages a low student-to-faculty ratio to ensure students receive a quality education and their success is paramount.
