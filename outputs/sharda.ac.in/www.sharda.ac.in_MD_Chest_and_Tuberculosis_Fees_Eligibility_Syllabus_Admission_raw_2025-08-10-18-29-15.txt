MD in TB and Chest programme at Sharda University is a three-year postgraduate programme designed in a way to train the next generation of medical leaders in pulmonary and critical care medicine. The programme aims to prepare students in internal medicine primarily specialising in preventing, diagnosing and treating conditions that affect lungs, bronchial tubes and respiratory system including the nose, pharynx and throat.
The curriculum is a blend of theoretical and practical components of the study. The goal of Post graduation (MD) programme in TB and Chest is to produce competent medical physicians who recognise the health needs of patients having chest complaints and carry out
professional obligations ethically along with having all the competencies required into chest medicine. The expert and renowned faculty always ensure that efforts are made towards performing adequate research on respiratory ailments and that students spend sufficient time in addressing pulmonary problems of public health in the country.
This course is for individuals who...
are looking forward to performing relevant investigative and therapeutic procedures for the patient suffering from chest and respiratory ailments and recognise the critical importance of pulmonary medicine in the context of the health priority of the country. Excellent communication skills with networking skills are added skills that can ensure success in this field.
Students who are looking for...
Acquiring the competencies pertaining to chest medicine that is required at all levels of the health care system. Anyone with a vision to be the leader in the medical field and want to give back to the society through their diagnostic, therapeutic and rehabilitative services should opt for this programme.
