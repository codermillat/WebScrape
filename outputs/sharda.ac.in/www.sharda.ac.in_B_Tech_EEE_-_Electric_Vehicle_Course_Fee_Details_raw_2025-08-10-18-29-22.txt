B.Tech (EEE) - Electric Vehicle Technology
SET0404
4 Years
• Course Fee
B.Tech in Electric Vehicle Technology, a dynamic four-year course designed for those eager to dive into the evolving world of electric vehicles (EVs). This program covers all aspects of EV technology, from design and development to advanced systems like batteries and hybrid vehicles. You'll learn about motor design, control systems, power electronics, and the environmental impact of EVs. The course culminates in a hands-on capstone project, where you'll design and build a prototype electric vehicle, preparing you for a promising career in this rapidly growing field.
