Post Graduate Diploma in Yogic Science (PGDYSc)
SHS0139
1 Year
• Course Fee
The proposed Post Graduate Diploma in Yogic Science programme is designed to meet the growing demand for holistic health practices and wellness education in a rapidly evolving world. As the global focus on mental health, physical well-being, and stress management intensifies, there is a significant need for professionals who are well-versed in the science and philosophy of yoga. This programme will equip students with comprehensive knowledge in yogic practices, anatomy, physiology, and meditation, offering both theoretical grounding and practical expertise. By fostering a deeper understanding of yoga, the programme will contribute to the academic pursuit of students, providing them with an essential foundation for further education in health, wellness, or even allied medical fields.
In terms of employability, the Post Graduate Diploma in Yogic Science opens diverse career opportunities across wellness centers, yoga studios, health retreats, and even corporate environments where wellness programs are becoming increasingly popular. The curriculum, with its practical focus, will also prepare students to become yoga instructors, therapists, or wellness consultants. Graduates of this programme will not only have specialized knowledge but will also develop critical life skills such as mindfulness, leadership, and communication, which are highly valued across various industries.
Sharda University, guided by its vision to be a global center of learning promoting professional excellence and innovation, and its mission to create a stimulating and flexible learning environment. Sharda University is committed to holistic education that blends traditional wisdom with modern knowledge. The introduction of the Post Graduate Diploma in Yogic Science aligns seamlessly with these objectives, enhancing academic excellence in several key ways:
• Integration of Traditional Wisdom with Modern Education: By incorporating yogic principles into the curriculum, the programme bridges ancient practices with contemporary academic pursuits, fostering a well-rounded educational experience.
• Promotion of Holistic Well-being: Emphasizing mental, physical, and spiritual health, the programme supports Sharda University's commitment to nurturing individuals capable of addressing societal challenges through critical thinking and innovative solutions.
• Encouragement of Research and Innovation: The study of yogic science opens avenues for research in interdisciplinary fields, encouraging students and faculty to explore new dimensions of health and wellness, thus contributing to the university's culture of innovation.
• Global Perspective and Cultural Diversity: Reflecting the university's core value of embracing diversity, the programme attracts students from various backgrounds, promoting cross-cultural understanding and preparing graduates for global citizenship.
Incorporating the Post Graduate Diploma in Yogic Science not only enriches Sharda University's academic offerings but also reinforces its mission to provide education that addresses the evolving needs of society.
