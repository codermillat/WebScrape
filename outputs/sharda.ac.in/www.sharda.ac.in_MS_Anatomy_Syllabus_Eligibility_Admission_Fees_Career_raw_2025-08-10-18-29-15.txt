3 Years
• Course Fee
MS Anatomy is a three-year post-graduate programme committed to making a significant contribution to science and delivering a high-quality education to the students. The programme deals with the study of human structure and life sciences. The coursework of the programme covers subjects like Human Gross Anatomy, Embryology and History of Anatomy, Histology, Neuroanatomy, Genetics and Physical Anthropology and Applied Anatomy & Recent Advances in Anatomy.
Under this programme, aspirants learn how to maintain and restore stable conditions in patients through pain management. In addition, they also gain hands-on industry experience in Sharda's Parent Hospital. Final assessments are made based on their performances. Graduates of this programme will be able to contribute to evaluation, planning and implementation at different levels of the healthcare system.
This course is for individuals who...
possess critical thinking skills to make informed patient care decisions. It is for candidates who have a strong mind and are compassionate towards others.
Students who are looking for...
obtaining a fulfilling career dedicated to patient care must opt for this programme.
