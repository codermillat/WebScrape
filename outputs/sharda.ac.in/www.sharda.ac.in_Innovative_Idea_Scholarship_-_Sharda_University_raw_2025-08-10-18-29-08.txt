Sharda University is proud to be a stakeholder in the Hon'ble Prime Minister's vision of self-reliant India. Infact, Sharda University's core ideology is to nurture more job creators than job seekers. We invite UG/PG students with Research & Entrepreneurial mindset to share their StartUp Idea. Besides giving upto 100% Scholarship & Seed Money, Sharda Launchpad Federation will nurture the innovative idea to success. Share your ideas at ideas.scholarship@sharda.ac.in
So far, 150 Sharda alumni have become entrepreneurs with business operations in diverse sectors. Sharda Launchpad's mission is well recognised by the Govt. of India and eight of its incubates have been selected to be assisted by INVEST INDIA - National Investment Promotion & Facilitation Agency, Ministry of Commerce and Industry, Government of India.
