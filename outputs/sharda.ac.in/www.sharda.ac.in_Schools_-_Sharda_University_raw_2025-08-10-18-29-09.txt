Teaching at Sharda University is organised across 14 schools which comprise of a broad range of disciplines. These 14 schools of studies, Doctoral and research, have an interdisciplinary approach to education and drive towards development in research and teaching in comparison to any university in India through a wide range of academic programs.
The sheer size and scale of the university originate exceptional opportunities for interdisciplinary ventures and collaborative working, facilitating both academic staff and students with opportunities to achieve great things. The teachers, researchers and students are active and involved in international collaborations in their respective disciplines focused on finding answers to the concerns our society and economy is facing today and are focused on paving the way for solutions to complex global issues impacting upon health, environment, technology and culture.
