Nursing
The post-graduate nursing programmes at Sharda University encourage students to acquire the knowledge, skills, and expertise required to become an advanced practice nurse. Under these programmes, students learn the most relevant theories and the latest skills from health and nursing professionals. The coursework of the programmes is regularly updated to include topical and innovative study areas, which mean students are equipped with the latest developments in the industry.
Students benefit from these programmes as a problem-based approach to learning is followed and integrated with clinical practice. In addition, candidates get an opportunity to study with a diverse cohort of nursing professionals and learn from their knowledge and experience. These post-graduate programmes are designed for students who have completed an undergraduate degree and who can meet minimum GPA requirements.
• M.Sc. (Nursing)- Community Health Nursing
• M.Sc. Psychiatric Nursing
• M.Sc. (Nursing)- Child Health (Pediatric) Nursing
• M.Sc. (Nursing)- Obstetrics & Gynecology
• M.Sc. (Nursing)- Medical Surgical Nursing
