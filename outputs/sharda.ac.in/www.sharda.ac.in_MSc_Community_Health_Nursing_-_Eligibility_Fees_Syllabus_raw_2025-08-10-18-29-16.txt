M.Sc. (Nursing)- Community Health Nursing
Sharda School of Nursing Science & Research (SNS&R)
SNS0105
2 Years
• Course Fee
The course is designed to assist students in developing expertise and in-depth understanding in the field of Community Health Nursing. It would help students to appreciate the holistic life style of individuals, families & groups and develop skills to function as Community Health Nurse specialists/practitioners. It would further enable students to function as an educator, managers and researcher in the field of Community Health Nursing
This course is for individuals who...
Exhibit the qualities of punctuality compassion and care. It is for students who seek satisfaction in improving patients lives and seek challenging career opportunities through experiential research.
Students who are looking for...
A fulfilling career dedicated to patients through by deploying their intelligent and care-giving potential
