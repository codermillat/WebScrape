BBA Logistics & Supply Chain Management (Hons./by Research)
4 Years
• Course Fee
BBA Logistics & Supply Chain Management (Hons./by Research) is a 4 years professional undergraduate program in business management that is exclusively designed to prepare students for managerial positions and entrepreneurship. BBA program provides knowledge as well as training in management and leadership abilities. The program outline is prepared to offer a perfect blend of theoretical and practical knowledge of subjects likes marketing, international business, finance, economics, hr, operations management.
Throughout this program students are encouraged to learn various aspects of business administration and management through classroom lectures, case studies and practical projects like internships. Regular industrial visits, guest lectures and workshops are planned to provide hands on training to students. Extraordinary measures are taken to improve the speech and communication skills of the students.
After completion of the program, students can decide on continuing their academic education and opt for a master’s degree program in business administration (MBA).
This course is for individuals who...
Individuals who have deep interest and aptitude to build a career in the management or business fields or starting their own venture.
Students who are looking for...
A career in management and business organizations where they can fully utilize their analytical and management skills must opt for this programme.
