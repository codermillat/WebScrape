Galgotias University, part of the Galgotias Education Group which was established in 1933, has a rich legacy of providing quality education in India. Since its inception, the university has been dedicated to delivering academic excellence to its students. Recognized by the University Grants Commission (UGC), Galgotias University is spread across a 52-acre state-of-the-art campus that boasts facilities comparable to the best universities in the world.
Galgotias University is committed to offering world-class education and research opportunities that are on par with international standards. With its highly qualified faculty members, cutting-edge facilities, and top-notch academic programs, Galgotias University is on track to becoming a globally recognized institution of higher learning that nurtures human resources to contribute to the community and develop science.
The university takes pride in its 'students-first' philosophy, which has helped it achieve consistently high rankings among India's top universities. With a low student-to-faculty ratio, the focus is on providing personalized attention and mentoring to each student. Galgotias University aims to produce graduates who not only have in-depth knowledge of their fields but also possess excellent problem-solving skills, a keen awareness of the societal context of their chosen profession, the ability to work effectively in teams, strong communication skills, and a commitment to lifelong learning.
Since the first batch of students was admitted in July 2011, Galgotias University has grown to accommodate more than 25,000 students in a diverse range of programs, including polytechnic, undergraduate, postgraduate, and PhD courses. The university has approvals from various councils, including the Pharmacy Council of India (PCI), Bar Council of India (BCI), Indian Nursing Council (INC), National Council for Teacher Education (NCTE), and Norms of National Council for Hotel Management (NCHM), among others.
Galgotias University prides itself on its record-breaking placements, which are a testament to its focus on maintaining the highest academic standards. The university is committed to providing its students with the best possible education and opportunities for personal and professional growth. With its multidisciplinary and interdisciplinary approach to education and research, Galgotias University is poised to become a world-class institution that produces globally competitive graduates capable of becoming leaders in their fields.
