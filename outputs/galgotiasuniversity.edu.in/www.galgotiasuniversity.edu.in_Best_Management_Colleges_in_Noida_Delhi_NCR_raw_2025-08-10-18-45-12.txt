The Galgotias University School of Business provides a cutting-edge business education where highly qualified faculty members prepare students for the rapidly evolving world of business. The School actively recruits top academics from around the world who are passionate about mentoring future leaders. Galgotias offers students an unparalleled opportunity to learn through partnerships with leading global universities like Purdue University, University of Arkansas, Fayetteville, University of Maryland, Kent State University, Northern Illinois University, Goethe University, Frankfurt, and Chifley Business School, among others. This allows students to benefit from a truly global learning environment, with country-specific case studies that give them a competitive advantage in their careers.
The School of Business incorporates lectures, tutorials, seminars, presentations, intensive workshops, and fieldwork as part of its teaching methods. Students benefit from a flexible credit system that allows them to take advantage of diverse educational offerings and pursue their own academic interests.
The School offers state-of-the-art facilities, including centrally air-conditioned buildings and classrooms, a fully wi-fi environment, state-of-the-art computer labs, well-equipped libraries, on-campus cafeterias, and sports facilities. Galgotias University School of Business has a great reputation for its top placements across diverse sectors and has established relationships with some of the biggest names in business for employment and internship opportunities.
Galgotias University has partnered with NSE Academy Limited to launch an NSE Smart Fin Lab, which includes NSMART, a simulated market trading application. This tool helps students gain practical trading experience on NSE's real-time data by trading on the NSMART platform without any financial risk. It provides access to learning methodologies that extend far beyond traditional forms of finance education, with technical charting tools, price feed simulators, and price feeds straight from NSE Trading session.
Additionally, the School of Business offers many value-added programs, such as Yoga Skills for Self Management, Aptitude Building and Logical Reasoning, and Stress Management. The missions for BBA, IBBA + MBA (Integrated BBA + MBA), and MBA (Dual Specialisation) Programs are to equip students with knowledge of various functional areas in management through Corporate World Interface, collaborate with industry for curriculum development and pedagogy, develop faculty by equipping them with teaching management techniques and interdisciplinary research, and develop business leaders with an ethical mindset capable of creating value for stakeholders and society.
